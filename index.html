What Mindvalley U actually is

An environment engineered for the conversations that change your life.

Each year, Mindvalley takes over one European city for 14 days. Mornings begin with yoga, breathwork, or a run through old town. Days are spent in keynotes, deep dives, and masterminds with the world's most sought-after teachers.

Evenings are dinners, fireside stories, rooftop parties — and the late-night conversations that go further than any of us planned. It's not a conference. It's not a retreat. It's a campus — for adults who believe who they're around determines who they become.

FAQ

Practical questions,
quickly answered.

01
Can I get a refund if I change my mind?
Yes. We offer flexible refunds if your plans change — book with confidence. See the tickets page for the full refund window and terms.
02
Can I attend for just one week?
Yes. 1-week tickets for Week 1 (Jul 20–26) or Week 2 (Jul 27–Aug 2), or the full 14 days. The schedule is deliberately flexible.
03
What is your cancellation policy?
Flexible cancellation options are available up to a specified date before the event. Please see the ticket page for the full policy. Our Connection Guarantee reflects the core promise of MVU — the room will meet you if you show up with an open mind.
04
Can my children attend?
Yes. Youth programs serve ages 3–17 parallel to the main event. Kids must be accompanied by a parent or guardian with a ticket. Youth tickets sold separately.
05
How does Community Stage selection work?
Once you have a ticket, you're eligible to apply. Mindvalley selects speakers based on the truth and transformation behind the message — not follower count.
06
Are payment plans available?
Yes — 2 and 4-payment installment options at checkout. Zero-interest split pay for all tiers.
